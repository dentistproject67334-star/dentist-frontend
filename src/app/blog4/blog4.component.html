Braces vs. Aligners

Which orthodontic option is best for you? Compare benefits & results.

Orthodontic treatment has evolved with options like traditional braces and clear aligners. Choosing the right method depends on your lifestyle, teeth alignment, and desired results.

1. Traditional Braces

Braces use metal brackets and wires to gradually move teeth into the correct position. They are highly effective for complex alignment issues.

2. Clear Aligners

Aligners are transparent, removable trays that gradually adjust teeth. They are discreet and convenient, ideal for mild to moderate cases.

3. Treatment Duration

Braces usually take 18-24 months for complete results, whereas aligners may take 12-18 months depending on the severity of misalignment.

4. Comfort & Lifestyle

Aligners are removable, making eating and oral hygiene easier. Braces may cause minor discomfort but offer predictable results for all types of cases.

5. Cost Considerations

Costs vary depending on the treatment complexity and materials used. Aligners may be slightly more expensive but offer greater convenience and aesthetics.
