Root Canal Myths Busted

Understand the truth about pain-free modern root canal treatments

Root canal treatment is often misunderstood, causing unnecessary fear. Modern techniques ensure a comfortable, pain-free experience while saving your natural teeth.

1. Myth: Root Canals Are Painful

With today’s anesthesia and advanced equipment, root canals are usually no more uncomfortable than a standard filling.

2. Myth: Extraction is Better Than a Root Canal

Saving your natural tooth with a root canal maintains jaw structure and prevents future dental complications.

3. Myth: Root Canals Require Multiple Visits

Many root canals can now be completed in a single visit using efficient tools and techniques.

4. Myth: Root Canal Teeth Are Weak

Once restored with a crown, a treated tooth is as strong as a natural one and can last a lifetime with proper care.

5. Myth: Only Severe Pain Means You Need a Root Canal

Early infection may cause mild discomfort or sensitivity. Routine check-ups help detect issues before pain becomes severe.
